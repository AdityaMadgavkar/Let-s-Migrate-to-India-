Education in India


1. Mandatory Education                             
  • Every child has to pass class 10th to get employment or higher studies.
  • As a result, all poor children get equal opportunity to learn in school.


2. Fee Structure                                         
  • The Fee structure of Indian schools is very much affordable when compared to other schools in the world.
  • Education fees tend to start from as low as USD 400 (31,794.38 Indian Rupees)


3. Extra Curriculars                                    
  • The modern curriculum also tends to focus more on acquiring skills in technology, communication, critical-thinking and problem solving.
  • The equal emphasis on extracurricular activities and co-curricular activities help build a student's personality and work upon his/her innate strengths.
  • There is emphasis on cultural studies and society.


Challenges                                                 
  • Different Language and Curriculum.
  • Difficulty in finding universities with suitable fees.
