Indian Cuisine

    1. rice, millets, pulses, coconut, are some of the crops grown in coastal plains of India
    2. Bajra, guar, jowar, maize, sesame, and groundnuts are the main crops in the Indian Desert.

2. Climate                                                   
  • Tropical wet, Tropical dry, Subtropical humid climate of India makes is suitable for growing large-scale crops.
  • India, being a peninsula, has a good supply of water; helping it to grow crops like rice.


3. Famous Dishes                                       
  • Tandoori Chicken: The chicken is marinated in ingredients such as yogurt, ginger, garlic, and herbs, and then baked in a clay oven.

  • Biryani: fluffy rice with vegetables and sometimes meats, making it a favorite for vegetarians and meat lovers alike.

  • Palak Paneer: A bowl of fresh palak and grated paneer tossed with spices and served with hot rotis.
