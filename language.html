Languages in India


1. Popularity                                                
  • Kannada language has the second oldest written tradition of all vernacular languages of India.
  • Hindi is the 2nd most spoken language in the world (ahead of English and Spanish)


2. Diversity                                                  
  • All Indian languages fall into one of these 4 groups: Indo-Aryan, Dravidian, Sino-Tibetan and Afro-Asiatic.
  • India's linguistic diversity consists of 1652 mother tongues.


3. Majority                                                   
  • The Indo-European languages are majorly spoken by 78.05% of Indians.
  • The official language of the Union Government of the Republic of India is Hindi
